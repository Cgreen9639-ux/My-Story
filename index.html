My Story

Introduction

My name is Cameron Earl Greene and I was a small kid from Elkridge, MD around rural nice Middle Class Folk. I had the capability to learn a lot and to learn from my father in business as well as military, political, & scientific history. Years later, I made the best decision of my life which was to go to the honors college of UMBC where I learned how to design, code on the front end, and full stack APIs.

My Childhood

  • I played for the Elkridge Hurricanes with Coach Tommy Gibson and a team of athletes that were a year older than me.
  • I learned principles like hard work, leadership, & communication.
  • My parents got divorced, which made me grow up quickly at the age of seven.
  • As a teenager, I faced many challenges, including being kicked out of my parents' houses.

Reality Set In

I was a sheltered 21-year-old college student that had to adapt my academic smarts into "street smarts." I faced many dangerous situations but trusted in the Lord to guide me through.

Finishing College
